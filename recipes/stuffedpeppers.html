Stuffed Peppers


Description

Hearty and classic stuffed pepper recipe

Ingredients

  • 4-6 Bell peppers
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 medium onion- chopped
  • 1 jalpeno pepper- chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic- chopped
  • 1 14.5oz can fire roasted tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper- to taste
  • 1 cup cooked wild rice

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425F and bring a pot of water to a boil
  2. Wash bell peppers. Cut off tops then scoop out insides. Retain tops
  3. Blanch bell peppers for 5 minutes to slightly soften
  4. Set softened peppers into a lightly oiled baking dish
  5. Heat oil in a large pan to medium and add the onions, chopped pepper tops, and jalapeno. Cook for 5 minutes to soften.
  6. Add the garlic and cook 1 minute, or until the garlic becomes fragrant
  7. Add the ground turkey and cook 5-6 minutes, breaking apart as you brown
  8. Add the fire roasted tomatoes, seasonings, and cooked rice. Stir together and remove from heat.
  9. Stir in half of the shredded cheese until incorporated
  10. Stuff each pepper with the meat filling mixture, then top each with the remaining shredded cheese
  11. Roast the stuffed peppers for 15-20 minutes, or until tender
