Beef Stew


Description

A hearty, warming beef stew recipe, perfect to warm up after a tiring winter day.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb beef chuck roast- cut to 2in chunks
  • 1 tsp kosher salt- to taste
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp gluten-free all purpose flour- sub. whole wheat
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 large yellow onion- chopped
  • 1 large carrot- cut into 2in chunks
  • 2 Yukon Gold potatoes- diced into 2in pieces
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme leaves- garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Season 2lb chuck roast with kosher salt and black pepper. Toss to coat in flour
  3. Heat olive oil in large dutch oven over medium heat. Brown beef in batches- 3-4 minutes per batch.- Transfer and set aside
  4. Add onion, garlic, and carrot to dutch oven. Stir occastionally and cook 2-3 minutes, or until lightly browned
  5. Add potatotes, beef broth, tomato paste, bay leaf, thyme leaves, and worcestershire sauce. Use wooden spoon to scrape fonde
  6. Stir while bringing mixture to simmer, return beef to pot. Cover and transfer to preheated oven
  7. Cook 2-2.5 hours. Remove bay leaf. Season if necessary
